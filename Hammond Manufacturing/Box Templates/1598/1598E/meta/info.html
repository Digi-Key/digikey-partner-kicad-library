Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1598E:
Black: 1598REBK - HM2075-ND
Black, Gray: 1598ESGYPBK - HM4077-ND
Gray: 1598ESGY - HM249-ND
Gray: 1598EGY - HM175-ND
Black: 1598EBK - HM166-ND


Features:
  • Desk top electronic instrument enclosure, ideally suited for mounting printed circuit boards.
  • Designed to meet IP54.
  • Two sets of vertical 1.5 mm (0.062”) P.C. board guides are molded into case halves front and rear.
  • Integral horizontal P.C. card mounts on Eurocard centers.
  • P.C. card standoffs molded into top & bottom half of enclosure - require #6 x 1/4” (6.4mm) screws - part number 1598ATS100

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
