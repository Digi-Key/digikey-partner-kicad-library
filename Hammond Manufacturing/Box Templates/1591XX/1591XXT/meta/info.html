Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1591XXT:

Black: 1591XXTSFLBK - HM1548-ND
Black: 1591XXTSBK - HM3995-ND
Gray: 1591XXTGY - HM1043-ND

Black: 1591XXTFLBK - HM3993-ND
Black: 1591XXTBK - HM1042-ND


Features:
  • Ideally suited for mounting printed circuit boards horizontally.
  • Tongue and groove construction provides protection against access of dust and splashing water.
  • Designed to meet IP54.
  • Lid and box both contain molded in PC board standoffs.
  • Assembly hardware included.

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
