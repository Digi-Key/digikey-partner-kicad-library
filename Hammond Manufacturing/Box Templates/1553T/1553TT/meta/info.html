Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Mfg 1553TT boxes:
Black: 1553TTBK - HM1342-ND
Gray: 1553TTGY - HM1344-ND
  • Ergonomically designed for hand-held comfort.
  • Two color combinations.
  • Molded from flame retardant ABS plastic, choice of black or light gray - material carries a UL flammability rating of 94V-0
  • Top cover is recessed for membrane display or keypad.
  • P.C. board standoffs are molded into the case top & bottom halves.
  • Integral brass bushings are factory inserted into top half of the enclosure.

Non-battery version has two removable end panels.
The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline & holes to align with board standoffs.
