Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1551USB2:
Translucent - Smoke: 1551USB2TSK - HM1469-ND
Translucent - Red: 1551USB2TRD - HM1470-ND
Gray: 1551USB2GY - HM1463-ND
Translucent - Clear: 1551USB2CLR - HM1462-ND
Black: 1551USB2BK - HM1461-ND


Features:
  • Ideally suited for dongles and/or mounting small printed circuit boards with USB connectors.
  • Molded in ABS plastic (material has a UL94-HB rating).
  • Available in five colors: in black, grey. translucent clear, translucent smoke and translucent red.
  • Satin texture finish.
  • Cover is recessed for easy label installation.
  • Complete with lid and two self-tapping screws.
  • P.C. board standoffs molded into interior
  • Designed to meet IP54.
  • For these same sizes without a USB cutout try our 1551MINI series.

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
