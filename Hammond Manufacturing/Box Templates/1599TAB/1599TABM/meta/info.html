Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Mfg 1599TABM boxes:
Black: 1599TABMBK - HM2086-ND
Gray: 1599TABMGY - HM2088-ND
  • Ergonomically designed for hand-held comfort.
  • Perfect for instrument or mobile data enclosures requiring a touch screen.
  • Molded from flame retardant ABS plastic, choice of black or grey - material carries a UL flammability rating of 94V-0.
  • Assembled with six (6) - M3-0.5 x 18 mm Phillips machine screws
  • P.C. board standoffs are molded into case top & bottom halves.
  • Bottom half of enclosure includes a square - molded inlay for labels.

Inside features PC board mounting posts.
The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline & holes to align with board standoffs.
