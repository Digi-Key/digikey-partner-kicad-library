Translucent - Blue: 1553BTBUBKBAT - HM1156-ND
Red: 1553BRDBKBAT - HM1174-ND

Black: 1553BBKBKBAT - HM1155-ND
Black: 1553BBKBAT - HM915-ND


Features:
  • Ergonomically designed for hand-held comfort.
  • Choice of five sizes and six color combinations.
  • Choice of two versions in the three larger sizes (ie...with or without battery door & compartment)
  • Molded in general purpose ABS plastic with an overmold, soft plastic grip.
  • Top cover is recessed for membrane display or keypad.
  • P.C. board standoffs are molded into case
  • Includes assembly hardware

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
