Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Mfg 1553A boxes:
Black: 1553ABK - HM395-ND
Gray: 1553AGY - HM396-ND
  • Ergonomically designed for hand-held comfort.
  • Two color combinations.
  • Molded in general purpose ABS plastic with an overmold, soft plastic grip.
  • Top cover is recessed for membrane display or keypad.
  • P.C. board standoffs are molded into case.
  • Gray 1553 series enclosures include nickel plated screws.  All other color use black screws.

Perfect for handheld use.
The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline & holes to align with board standoffs.
