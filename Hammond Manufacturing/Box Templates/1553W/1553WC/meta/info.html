Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1553WC:
Gray: 1553WCGY - HM1421-ND
Black: 1553WCBK - HM1420-ND


Features:
  • Ergonomically designed for hand-held comfort.
  • Designed to meet IP65.
  • Uses a one piece silicone gasket (installed). Enclosure utilizes tongue & groove construction - molded into the top & bottom halves.
  • Molded in flame retardant - ABS plastic (FRABS) - UV stabilized. With a grey overmold, soft plastic grip.
  • Top cover is recessed for membrane display or keypad.
  • P.C. board standoffs are molded into both the top & bottom of the enclosure (see drawings or photos for more details). Standoff screws (M2) not included.
  • Front panel is an integral part of the top half of the enclosure.
  • Enclosure is secured with black Philips, stainless steel, machine screws, threaded into integral brass bushings. Perfect for applications when repetitive assembly and disassembly are required.
  • Assembly hardware included

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
