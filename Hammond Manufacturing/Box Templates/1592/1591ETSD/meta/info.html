Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1591ETSD:
Gray: 1592ETSDGY - HM4003-ND
Black: 1592ETSDBK - HM4002-ND


Features:
  • Perfect for instrument or mobile data enclosures requiring a keypad and display.
  • Available with either a solid display area or an opening with a clear lens
  • Molded from flame retardant ABS plastic, choice of black or grey (material carries a UL flammability rating of 94V-0).
  • The back of the case has been designed to accept a magnetic strip or “hook & loop” fabric mounting tab.

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
