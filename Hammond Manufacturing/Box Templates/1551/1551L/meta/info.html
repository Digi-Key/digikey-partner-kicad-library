Project template containing the recommended board layout for Hammond Manufacturing 1551L:

Black: 1551LFLBK - HM1065-ND
Black: 1551LBK - HM909-ND


Features:
  • Ideally suited for mounting small printed circuit boards or used as potting boxes.
  • Molded in a choice of black, gray, or translucent blue general purpose ABS plastic.
  • Satin texture finish.
  • Complete with lid and two self tapping screws.
  • This series is also available with flanged lid or pre-installed key-rings.
  • 2 P.C. board standoffs molded into interior.
  • Lap joint construction provides protection against access of dust and splashing water.
  • Designed to meet IP54.

The "Dwgs.User" layer contains all critical dimmensions in milimeters[inches].
The "Edge.Cuts" layer contains the maximum board outline and holes to align with board standoffs.
